ParkRun Results Stats-er-rer bookmarklet

A simple bookmarklet to garner some stats from a ParkRun results page.

Once installed, go to a parkRun results page and click the bookmarklet to see:

  • Number of runners (and M/F split)
  • Total runs between them all
  • Number of PBs
  • Number of First timers
  • 50, 100, 250 club members

Installation

  1. Drag this link onto your bookmarks bar
  2. Go to a ParkRun results page such as http://www.parkrun.org.uk/gedling/results/latestresults/
  3. Click your new bookmarklet to see the stats
